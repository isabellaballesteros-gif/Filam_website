An interactive, multi-page data and narrative visualization by Isabella Ballesteros.

This visualization showcases the identity struggles of being Filipino American. Two opposite cultures in one person and how it affects mental health. After acknowledging the hardships, this project informs the viewer of data from the Filipino American population in the United States and a fun way to learn about an aspect of Filipino culture.
